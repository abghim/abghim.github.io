Hello, I'm Aiden Ghim.

Hi all, I am a student living in South Korea.

My goal is to appreciate my place in the universe. Little moments when I am synthesizing knowledge, reveling in beauty, or being lost amidst the vastness of the world add up to this. For me, those moments happen when I do math, physics, programming, stargazing, calligraphy, and reading.

In an attempt to hold on to those moments a little longer, I decided to write them down. To make those moments last forever, I decided to share them here.
